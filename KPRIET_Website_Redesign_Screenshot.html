Welcome to KPRIET

KPR Institute of Engineering and Technology is an Autonomous Engineering Institution established in 2009. The institution is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. The institution has been accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA) and National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with 'A+' Grade.
